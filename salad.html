SALLAD

Ingredients

  • grön
  • sallad
  • olja

Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and grated carrot. Cook until softened.

2. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.

3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato purée, and beef stock. Bring to a simmer.

4. Season with salt and pepper. Reduce the heat and let simmer for 30-40 minutes.

5. Serve over cooked spaghetti and enjoy!
